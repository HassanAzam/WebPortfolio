Outline

Irrigation water is a very precious resource and with passage of time decline in this resource has been expected due to rapid climate changes. Therefore efficient utilization of irrigation water has become extremely essential by considering future situations and realizing importance of this precious resource. Researchers and scientists can find ways for efficient utilization of water and even they can propose solutions for complete implementation of smart irrigation systems.

This Workshop has been organized to bring together researchers and scientists engaged in this interdisciplinary work and provide a forum for discussion and presenting new methods and results. Contributions are solicited for original research work illuminating the role of natural sciences and technology in Smart Irrigation, for example in areas such as:

  • Cutting Edge Techniques in Smart Irrigation and Water Resource Management

  • Agriculture Experts Perspective

  • WSNs for efficient utilization of water

  • IoT for enabling smart irrigation systems

  • Big data analysis and cloud computing

  • Farmers/customers perspective and
    awareness of technology


The workshop will be hosted by CIS department at NEDUET. The program will be structured around mentioned areas and consist of invited talks, contributed talks and poster sessions.

Call For Papers


For contributed talks and poster, interested researchers/scientists can send 2-page abstract, that will be reviewed by Advisory Board members who will make recommendations for oral or poster presentations. Deadline for abstract submission is 15th April 2016. Please note the instructions for authors here. For submitting your abstracts, please use the email address mkhurrum@neduet.edu.pk




Oral presentations


The lecture hall is equipped with a digital projector and a PC (MS Windows 7, Office 2010). It is recommended that you use Powerpoint. Presentations can have an aspect ratio up to 16x10.
i. Contributed oral presentations will have a gross duration of 30 min that breaks down to 20 min for presentation and 10 min for discussion.
ii. Invited oral presentations have a gross duration of 60 min that breaks down to 45 min for presentation and 15 min for discussion.



Poster presentations




Poster panels are suitable for A0 posters.
